Food

How much food is produced in NJ or the US? Food accounts for over a quarter (26%) of global greenhouse gas emissions(Ritchie).


Vegetables/Fruits

  • Brassicaceae [pronounced: brass-ih-KAY-see-ee ](broccoli, cauliflower)
  • Legumes(beans, lentils, peas)
  • Root Vegetable(carrots, potatoes, yams)

Nationally, New Jersey is one of the top 10 producers of blueberries, cranberries, peaches, tomatoes, bell peppers, eggplant"(NJDA).

Proteins/Meats

Consider the following.

  • Is it grassfed?
  • Where are you purchasing it from? Is it produced locally or in the USA?
